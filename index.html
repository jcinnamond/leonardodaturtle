Hello! Meet Leonardo da Turtle, the world's greatest digital artist. They might look like a badly drawn semicircle because I don't have the time or talent required to draw a turtle, but that's what all the digital artists are wearing these days.

Help Leonardo create their next great masterpiece by giving them instructions. You can tell them to move forward a specified number of pixels. You can tell them to turn left or right by a specified number of degrees. For example, to draw the letter "L" you can say 

forward 200
left 180
forward 200
left 90
forward 100

(You can give instructions one by one or put them all on one line. Leonardo isn't fussy.)
